Graduation Project

 The project began with the question: "Can we create a new place on the Han River through a pedestrian bridge?" The aim was to integrate experiences that can occur in waterfront spaces by spatially connecting the urban space along the Dongho Bridge riverside, the riverside park, and the Riverbus dock. By combining elements such as a pedestrian bridge, a station platform, and a dock, we propose a new pedestrian bridge that offers diverse access points and utilizes the potential of the Han River to create a new leisure facility.

 The fundamental direction of the design is to connect these various places through the Dongho Pedestrian Bridge, enhancing accessibility and proposing a new destination on the Han River.
