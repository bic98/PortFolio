Computational Projects

The activities listed below are related to computer science. In addition to studying architecture, my keen interest in computers led me to concurrently explore the field of computer science while attending university. I have a passion for solving problems using algorithms and discovering insights within big data to address various challenges. I've actively participated in related activities, taking pleasure in uncovering solutions and insights. My interest extends to integrating architecture and computer science to create more user-friendly programs, and I am particularly fond of using parametric design to develop designs that harmonize well with nature. If you have any questions about my work, please feel free to reach out through the "contact" section. I am committed to providing thoughtful and detailed responses.
