03_Extruding a quadrilateral polyline according to given conditions.

Key Ideas:

1. Create multiple curves of different scales and compare their distances from the center of the shape.

2. Perform a true/false check to determine if multiple curves of different scales intersect with each other.

Key Idea 1 : 1. Create multiple curves of different scales and compare their distances from the center of the shape.

You can branch shapes that come within a distance of 3 units from each curve.

Key Idea 2 : 2. Perform a true/false check to determine if multiple curves of different scales intersect with each other.

Dot Product Formula 1 :

The dot product of two vectors \( \vec{a} \) and \( \vec{b} \) can also be expressed as the sum of the products of their components:

$$ \vec{a} \cdot \vec{b} = a_1 \cdot b_1 + a_2 \cdot b_2 + \dots + a_n \cdot b_n $$


  • \( \vec{a} = \begin{pmatrix} a_1 \\ a_2 \\ \vdots \\ a_n \end{pmatrix} \) is the vector \( \vec{a} \).
  • \( \vec{b} = \begin{pmatrix} b_1 \\ b_2 \\ \vdots \\ b_n \end{pmatrix} \) is the vector \( \vec{b} \).


  • The dot product is the sum of the products of corresponding components.
